Necessary Elements of Any Location I Go To:

I have yet to travel to every city I plan to travel to, so I don’t want to declare a favorite place (Except for New York City; nothing could ever make me hate NYC). However, I have favorite characteristics of different locations I've seen online or once in real life.

Mountain Landscapes

Mountains are a necessity in the landscape of a location because they are so gorgeous and breath-taking. This year I went to Europe and saw the swiss alps and they were stunning. It was a bit cold but it was worth it for the mountains.

A Plethora of Cats

There should be cats wherever I go. (There's an exception if its a museum or something). Cats are my favorite animal. It doesn't matter how many cats are at that location, but I prefer many cats. Some cities have stray cats that are considered a part of their community, and the cats are fed and pet by random people who pass by.
I will be one of those random people that pass by to feed and pet the cats...

Delectable Food

There has to be good food at the location I travel to. I am very eager and willing to try new foods, but the foods I try must be awesome. (This is not really a big issue whenever I travel because food is delicious everywhere, but just in case.)

City Landscapes

If there are no mountains at a location or if the mountains are too far away to see, I would want to visit a city instead. Yes, I already live in a city, and I travel to Manhattan every day, but I still have yet to see every single acre of the city. I live in Queens and still haven’t seen every part of it.
